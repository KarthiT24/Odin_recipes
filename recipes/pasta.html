Pasta

Description

To make a delicious pasta dish, start by boiling 200 grams of your preferred pasta according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat and sauté 2 cloves of minced garlic and 1 chopped onion until translucent. Add 1 can of diced tomatoes, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes. For the vegetarian version, add 1 cup of chopped bell peppers, zucchini, and spinach, and cook until the vegetables are tender. For a non-vegetarian option, add 200 grams of cooked and sliced chicken breast or shrimp. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix well. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil before serving. Enjoy your flavorful vegetarian or non-vegetarian pasta dish!

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 200 grams of your preferred pasta

Basic Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Vegetarian Option

  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup spinach

Non-Vegetarian Option

  • 200 grams cooked and sliced chicken breast or shrimp

Steps

  1. Boil 200 grams of your preferred pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat and sauté 2 cloves of minced garlic and 1 chopped onion until translucent.
  3. Add 1 can of diced tomatoes, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. For the vegetarian option, add 1 cup of chopped bell peppers, zucchini, and spinach, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. For the non-vegetarian option, add 200 grams of cooked and sliced chicken breast or shrimp.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  7. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil before serving. Enjoy your flavorful pasta dish!
